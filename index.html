God prepared us, God was in the midst, God taught us

...so that all the peoples of the earth may know that the hand of the Lord is mighty...
Joshua 4:24

We’ve heard stories of God moving in mighty ways during the Coronavirus pandemic. There’s no way to know if it’s because we are paying more attention to His movements or because we are making room for Him to move. Maybe it is both. No matter the reason, there's a lot to record in this season:

  • The ways God prepared us for this time
    ...and who knows whether you have not come to the kingdom for such a time as this?
    Esther 4:14
  • The ways we saw God move in the midst of this pandemic
    God is our refuge and strength,
    a very present help in trouble.
    Psalm 46:1
  • What God taught us during this pandemic
    I will instruct you and teach you in the way you should go;
    I will counsel you with my eye upon you.
    Psalm 32:8

How can I share my story?

You can contribute your stories of God’s movement in your life during the pandemic by completing one or more of these statements:

  1. Looking back, I can see how God was preparing me for this time by…
  2. I saw God moving in the midst of this pandemic when…
  3. During the pandemic, God taught me...

Email your stories to stories@pandemicfaith.org

What is pandemicfaith.org about?

There will come a time when life returns to normal, first a “new normal” and then back to how life was before the pandemic. At first it will be easy to remember what life was like during this pandemic, but over time the memories may fade as the pandemic is written into history. We might forget how we saw God in the midst of this, what he taught us, and the eye-opening moments when we realized God was working in our lives to prepare us for such a time as this.

Before we forget, let’s record the signs of God’s mighty power, His lessons for us, and His revelations of His work that prepared us for a time like this. Looking back, we will be able to remember our stories and share them with future generations.
